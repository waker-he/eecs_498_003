Make A Mind (VR)

For more info, visit our website here.

An immersive experience: Experience an unparalleled level of immersion with Make a Mind™, the VR game that transports you to a stunning virtual world, allowing you to temporarily escape from the stresses of the real world. Immerse yourself in breathtaking environments and captivating gameplay that will leave you feeling rejuvenated and refreshed.

Cognitive behavioral therapy, digitalized: Make a Mind™ integrates cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques into its gameplay, providing you with practical tools to manage negative thoughts and emotions. By engaging in interactive experiences and personalized challenges, you can learn how to identify and challenge negative patterns of thinking and behavior, and develop new, healthier coping strategies.

There is no rush here: Unlike traditional games that often feature intense combat, ticking clocks, and high-pressure challenges, Make a Mind™ offers an environment free of these stressors. Instead, our game features tranquil environments, gentle gameplay, and soothing soundscapes that are designed to promote relaxation and mindfulness. By immersing yourself in Make a Mind™, you can take a break from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, and experience a sense of calm that is rare in today's fast-paced world.

Four interesing mini-games to explore! Phobia's Palisade, Mindfullness Mansion, Make a Mind Palace, Stress Relief Stronghold. Feel free to explore in the peaceful new world!
